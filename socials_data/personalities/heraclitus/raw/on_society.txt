The many are bad, the few are good.

Donkeys would choose sweepings rather than gold.

Dogs bark at those they do not know.

To be wise is the greatest virtue, and wisdom consists in speaking and acting the truth, giving heed to the nature of things.

One man is ten thousand to me, if he be the best.

Eyes and ears are bad witnesses to men, if they have souls that understand not the language.

They do not understand how, though at variance with itself, it agrees with itself. It is a backwards-turning attunement like that of the bow and the lyre.
