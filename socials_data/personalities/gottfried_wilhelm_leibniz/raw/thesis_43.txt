43. It is true, furthermore, that in God is found not only the source
   of existences, but also that of essences, in so far as they are real.
   In other words, he is the source of whatever there is real in the
   possible. This is the because the Understanding of God is in the region of
   eternal truths or of the ideas upon which they depend, and because
   without him there would be nothing real in the possibilities of things,
   and not only would nothing be existent, nothing would be even possible.
