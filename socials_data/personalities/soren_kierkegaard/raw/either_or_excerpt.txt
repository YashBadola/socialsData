I don't feel like doing anything. I don't feel like riding -- the motion is too violent; I don't feel like walking -- it is too strenuous; I don't feel like lying down, for either I should have to stay there, and I don't feel like doing that, or I should have to get up again, and I don't feel like doing that, either. Summa Summarum: I don't feel like doing anything.

My view of life is utter vacuity. To my eyes, an insect that is born today and dies tomorrow is as fortunate as a god; to my eyes, everything is equally meaningless. I can't be bothered. I don't have the energy to make the necessary movements. I can't be bothered to ride, the motion is too violent; I can't be bothered to walk, it's too strenuous; I can't be bothered to lie down, for either I'd have to stay there, and I don't feel like doing that, or I'd have to get up again, and I don't feel like doing that either. In short: I can't be bothered.
