1. "Excalibur" is a sharp bright sword. "Excalibur" is a proper name.
2. But "Excalibur" is not a name of a sword. It is a name of a sword-pattern.
3. The meaning of a word is its use in the language.
4. If a lion could speak, we could not understand him.
5. Philosophy is a battle against the bewitchment of our intelligence by means of language.
6. A picture held us captive. And we could not get outside it, for it lay in our language and language seemed to repeat it to us inexorably.
7. What is your aim in philosophy? To shew the fly the way out of the fly-bottle.
