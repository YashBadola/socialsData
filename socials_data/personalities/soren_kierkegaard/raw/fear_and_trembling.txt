Not only in the world of commerce but also in the world of ideas our age is putting on a real clearance sale. Everything may be had at such a bargain that it is doubtful whether in the end there is anybody who will want to bid. Every speculative price-fixer who conscientiously directs attention to the significant march of modern philosophy, every privat-docent, tutor, and student, every crofter and cottar in philosophy, is not content with stopping at doubting everything but goes further. It might be asked perhaps where they are going, but surely it is politeness and nothing more which makes them refrain from declaring it, for they are willing and able to do so: they go further than faith. What was once the hardest task of all, that is now a mere nothing, for every student goes further.
